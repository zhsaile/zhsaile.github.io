63. About the Study of Grammar

Firstly, don't look upon the study of grammar as all or nearly all that you have to do in learning to write correctly. 语法学习并不是学习如何正确写作的全部, 不应该把全部精力都投入到语法学习上来.

Secondly, don't indulge in much reasoning from the definitions, that would be sheer waste of time and energy. 不要沉溺于语法定义的推导

Thirdly, don't be a slave to grammatical terms, about which uniformity does not prevail. 在写作时不应该囿于语法条框, 在这些术语中, 统一性并不占据优势. (?)

Fourthly, don't draw hasty conclusions from one or two examples. 不要急于从一两个例子中总结规律.

Fifthly, don't cling to any narrowly grammatical point of vie at the expense of idiom. 不应该为了遵守某些狭隘的语法规定而放弃使用 idiom, as said before, when grammar and idiom do not agree, follow the idiom.

Sixthly, don't treat the study of grammar merely as something curious or fascination, though you may delight in the philosophy or science of the language. 纵然你很有可能在学习关于语言本身科学/哲学的过程中感到愉悦, 但是不要仅仅把语法学习视作满足好奇心或者是简单的迷恋.

Seventhly, don't content yourself with knowing the rules of the grammar. To know the grammar is one thing, to keep is another. 不要仅仅满足于对语法知识的了解, 而要在写作中尽量保持, 了解与掌握并在写作中保持完全是两回事.
